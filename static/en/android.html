How to Download YouTube Videos on Android

Downloading videos on Android is simple. Follow this straightforward guide to save videos directly to your device.

Step 1: Copy the Video Link

First, get the link from the YouTube app. Open the app and navigate to the video you wish to download.

  • Tap the "Share" button located below the video player.
  • From the options that appear, select "Copy link".

Illustration: Tapping 'Share' then 'Copy link' in the YouTube app on Android.

Step 2: Paste Link in Browser

Now, open your preferred web browser, such as Chrome, Firefox, or Samsung Internet.

  • Navigate to SafeYTDownloader.com.
  • Tap and hold the input field, then choose "Paste" to enter the copied link.

Illustration: Pasting the link into the downloader website on Chrome for Android.

Step 3: Start the Download

Once the link is pasted, you can begin processing and downloading the video file.

  • Click the "Process Now" button and agree to the terms.
  • Click the red "Agree" button to agree to the terms of use.

Illustration: Pressing process now and agreeing to terms of use.

Step 4: Find Your Video

Save the video on your phone.Then put it in your phone's gallery or download folder.

  • After processing, click the green "Download" button. Your browser will ask you to confirm the download.
  • Check your browser's "Downloads" section or open your phone's "Files" app and look in the "Downloads" folder.
  • Alternatively, open your "Gallery" or "Google Photos" app. The video will usually appear automatically in a "Downloads" album.

Illustration: Locating the saved video in the Google Photos app on Android.
