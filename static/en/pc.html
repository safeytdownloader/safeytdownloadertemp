How to Download YouTube Videos on PC

Downloading videos on a desktop computer (Windows, macOS, Linux) is fast and straightforward. Follow this simple guide.

Step 1: Copy the Video URL

Open your web browser, go to YouTube, and find the video you want to download.

  • Click on the address bar at the top of your browser to highlight the full URL.
  • Right-click and select "Copy", or use the keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+C on Windows, Cmd+C on Mac).

Illustration: Highlighting and copying the URL from the browser's address bar.

Step 2: Paste the Link

In a new tab or window, navigate to our website to begin the download process.

  • Go to SafeYTDownloader.com.
  • Paste the URL into the input field using your mouse (right-click and "Paste") or keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V).

Illustration: Pasting the link into the input field on SafeYTDownloader.

Step 3: Choose Format & Download

After pasting the link, click the process now button to begin processing.

  • Click the red "Process Now" button to process the video.
  • You will be Prompted with a term of use agreement. Press the red "Agree" button.

Illustration: Agreeing the to terms of use.

Step 4: Save and Locate File

Save the file. The video will be saved to your computer's default download location.

  • Click the green button that says Download:(youtube video name).mp4
  • Once the download is complete, you can find the video in your "Downloads" folder or the location you selected.

Illustration: Pressing the download button and the downloaded file in the folder.
